Crystal Collector!

These are the rules of the game.


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  2. There are four crystals below.
  3. Each crystal will be assigned a random value at the beginning of every game.
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click it.
  5. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  6. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
